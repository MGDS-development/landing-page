About

This website have been created in the context of the Madrid Green Data Space (MGDS) project, funded from September to December 2020 by EIT Digital under a joint Cross-KIC activity with EIT Climate-KIC.

The main objectives of this project included the generation of a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) for a data space associated to the EIT Climate-KIC-funded Madrid Health, Clean Cities Deep Demonstration, as well as a dashboard related to different aspects of the green infrastructure of the city of Madrid, providing digital support to some of the current experiments in the experiment portfolio.

We have worked on the deployment of such a data space, following the main architectural components of the IDSA reference architecture. As a result, the current data asset catalogue is available here. We have also worked on the generation and exploitation of several green-infrastructure-related datasets, including open data published by the city of Madrid, by the Instituto Geográfico Nacional and by Copernicus, generating derived data sources for the city of Madrid according to a set of well-known indicators around green infrastructure (NDVI, Green Space Index, Green Spaces Proximity Index, Green Space Density and Green-Space Built-Space Ratio), which are made available with a XXXXXXXXX license. The results can be visualized and navigated via a dashboard. A continuously evolving beta version of this dashboard is available here.
